🚀 Getting Started

Thank you for downloading Booksurf. This extension helps you organize and quickly access your bookmarks with a beautiful, customizable interface.

Quick Start Guide:

  1. Use the Booksurf Manager to organize your bookmarks.
  2. Customize the appearance in Settings.
  3. Switch between Grid and List views.
  4. Use the search bar to quickly find bookmarks.

📁 Bookmark Management

Booksurf integrates seamlessly with Chrome's native bookmark system. You can create folders, drag and drop to reorder, and edit titles or URLs directly from the extension.

Changes made in Booksurf are instantly synced with your Chrome profile.

📐 Views & Layout

Choose how your bookmarks are displayed:

  • Grid View: A beautiful speed-dial layout with large favicons. Perfect for visual browsing.
  • List View: A compact, text-heavy layout. Ideal if you have hundreds of bookmarks.

Adjust columns, spacing, and alignment in Settings → Display.

🎭 Presets & Themes

Save your custom configurations as presets. Booksurf includes several built-in themes, and you can create up to 10 custom presets.

Presets save your background, typography, card styles, and animation settings. Export them as JSON to share with friends.

🖼️ Backgrounds & Textures

Personalize your new tab page with custom backgrounds:

  • Images: Upload your own photos (Max: 5 MB). Images are compressed and cached locally.
  • Textures: Choose from built-in CSS patterns and gradients.

Note: Switching from an image to a texture will delete the uploaded image to save storage.

🃏 Cards & Typography

Control exactly how your bookmark cards look:

  • Favicons: Adjust size, shape (rounded/square), and quality.
  • Typography: Change font family, size, weight, and color.
  • Card Styles: Toggle backgrounds, borders, and hover effects.

🎛️ UI & Header Styling

Fine-tune the extension's interface:

  • Header: Adjust transparency, blur effect, and height.
  • Search Bar: Customize width, placeholder text, and position.
  • Scrollbars: Hide or style for a cleaner look.

✨ Animations & Effects

Bring your speed-dial to life with smooth animations:

  • Entry Effects: Fade, Slide, Scale, or Bounce on page load.
  • Stagger Delay: Control delay between each card's animation.
  • Hover Effects: Lift, glow, or scale on hover.

🔍 Search & Navigation

The search bar is incredibly fast and searches across:

  • Bookmark Titles
  • URLs
  • Folder Names

Use Enter to open the first result, or Arrow Keys to navigate before pressing Enter.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Power users can navigate Booksurf without a mouse:

  • Ctrl + K (or Cmd + K): Focus Search Bar
  • Enter: Open selected bookmark
  • Esc: Clear search / Close menus
  • ↑ ↓ ← →: Navigate bookmarks

📦 Import & Export

Booksurf supports flexible data management:

  • Bookmarks: Export as JSON, CSV, or HTML.
  • Folders: Export specific folders as JSON to share.
  • Settings: Export custom presets via Settings → Presets → Export.

💾 Cache & Performance

To ensure fast load times, Booksurf caches favicons and background images locally.

If you notice slow load times, navigate to Settings → Cache Management and click Clear Cache. This won't delete your bookmarks.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Background image won't upload

Chrome cache may be full. Clear the cache in Settings and try a smaller image (under 5 MB).

Importing bookmarks failed

The import file may be corrupt. Try using your browser's built-in bookmark manager instead.

Favicons load slowly or are missing

Navigate to Settings → Cache Management and clear the cache to force a fresh download.

Storage is full

Clear the cache and consider removing unused custom presets.
