FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to help you get started.

Background image won't upload

Chrome cache may be full. Clear the cache in Settings and try a smaller image (under 5 MB).

Importing bookmarks failed

The import file may be corrupt. Try using your browser's built-in bookmark manager instead.

Favicons load slowly or are missing

Navigate to Settings → Cache Management and clear the cache to force a fresh download.

Storage is full

Clear the cache via Settings → Cache Management and remove unused custom presets.
