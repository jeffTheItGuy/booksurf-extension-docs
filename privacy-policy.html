Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Thank you for choosing Booksurf. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Data Collection and Use

What We Collect

Booksurf collects and processes the following data:

  1. Bookmarks Data: We access your Chrome bookmarks to display, organize, and manage them within the extension. This includes:
    • Bookmark titles
    • Bookmark URLs
    • Folder structure
    • Date added/modified
  2. Local Storage: The extension stores data locally in your browser:
    • Custom settings and preferences
    • Cached favicons (website icons)
    • Uploaded background images (max 5 MB)
    • Custom presets and themes
  3. Usage Data: We do NOT collect any analytics, usage statistics, or browsing history.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display and organize your bookmarks
  • Provide customization features (themes, backgrounds, layouts)
  • Cache favicons for faster loading
  • Save your preferences between sessions

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

All data is stored locally on your device. Booksurf does NOT:

  • Transmit your bookmarks to any external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Collect personal information beyond what's necessary for bookmark management

Chrome Sync

If you enable Chrome sync, your bookmarks may be synced across your devices through Google's services, subject to Google's privacy policy.

Permissions

Booksurf requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • bookmarks: To read, create, update, and organize your bookmarks
  • storage: To save your settings and preferences locally
  • favicon: To display website icons for your bookmarks
  • tabs: To open bookmarks in new tabs

These permissions are necessary for the core functionality of the extension.

Third-Party Services

Booksurf does NOT integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Retention

  • Bookmarks: Stored as long as you keep them in Chrome
  • Settings & Preferences: Stored indefinitely until you clear them
  • Cached Favicons: Stored until cache is cleared or updated
  • Background Images: Stored until you change or delete them

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the extension
  • Delete any cached data via Settings → Cache Management
  • Export your bookmarks and settings at any time
  • Uninstall the extension, which will remove all locally stored data (your Chrome bookmarks remain intact)

Children's Privacy

Booksurf does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us.
